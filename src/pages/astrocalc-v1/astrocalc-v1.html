What does it do?

Conventional calculators offer basic, scientific, finance, and programming modes but none offer an astronomy mode. AstroCalc attempts to fill the gap. Version 1 is capable of transforming catalog coordinates (i.e. RA and DEC) for any object into horizontal coordinates (i.e. AZ and ALT) for any observer and time. More functionality to come!
